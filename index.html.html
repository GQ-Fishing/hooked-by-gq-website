About Us

Two Friends. One Passion. Countless Adventures.

Meet Glenn & Quentin

Hooked By GQ was created by Glenn and Quentin, two friends brought together by a shared love for fishing and the outdoors. What started as casual fishing trips quickly turned into a passion for creating content and sharing real, exciting fishing experiences with others.

On the Hooked By GQ YouTube channel, Glenn and Quentin document their fishing adventures, from landing unforgettable catches to learning new techniques and enjoying time on the water. Their goal is to keep things real—showing both the wins and the challenges that come with fishing—while keeping the content entertaining and easy to enjoy.

Hooked By GQ is all about having fun, respecting nature, and building a community of people who love fishing as much as they do. Whether you're an experienced angler or just getting started, the channel is a place to learn, laugh, and stay hooked.

Thanks for being part of the Hooked By GQ journey.
